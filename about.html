About the Registry

The Global Trader Registry provides institutional-grade credential verification for financial market professionals worldwide.

Our Mission

In modern financial markets, trust must be verified. Our mission is to maintain a definitive, publicly accessible ledger of certified trading professionals. By centralizing license statuses, historical verification dates, and professional credentials, we protect market integrity and prevent institutional fraud.

The registry serves as the primary lookup point for compliance officers, prime brokers, and counterparties who need to confirm a trader's standing before engaging in business.

How Verification Works

Every trader listed in the registry has been cross-referenced against regulatory filings, licensing bodies, and continuing-education records. A record's status — Verified & Active, Expired, or Suspended — reflects the most recent compliance check on file.

Searching by Trader ID or License Number returns the professional's identity, licensing details, registration timeline, certifications, and authorized trading categories, along with a scannable verification code and downloadable certificate.

Compliance & Data Protection

The registry publishes only the information necessary to confirm a trader's professional standing. We do not disclose contact details, trading positions, or financial account information. All published data is handled in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.
